Dragon project revision & reflection

After having my fiancé review my project dragon website she liked the site and found it easy to navigate and that it worked well especially since I had no experience with web design prior to this course. She did suggest some minor a few minor changes the main one being the addition of a slideshow or something more interactive on the main page. Also she was not a big fan of my main color scheme. I decided to add the slide show that we went through in class to the bottom of my page but I left the color scheme because I liked it and I felt it made the site easy to read.
